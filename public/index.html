Learn about the station from NASA, ESA, and Wikipedia and join us in development with your ideas and corrections via Trello and pull requests via GitHub, or discuss the telemetry or development in our Slack team.

Development by Matt and Yuri of the SenseData Group; inspired by the 2015 Space Apps Challenge; written in ES6 via Babel; and built on Bootstrap, Crossfilter, D3, lodash, Moment.js, io.js, PostgreSQL, React, Socket.IO, and Three.js; hosted on Heroku and Amazon RDS.
